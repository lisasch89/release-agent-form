An AI-generated customer-facing release note will be created as a draft in the Freshdesk Product News folder. It is NOT published automatically. Customer-facing content always needs human review.

Your next steps:
  1. Go to the Product News folder in Freshdesk and open the draft.
  2. Review the content carefully. Polish or correct anything that needs improving so it reads well to customers.
  3. Send the draft to the Customer team for final review and publishing.
The Customer team only reviews drafts that you actively send to them. They will not check Freshdesk on their own.
